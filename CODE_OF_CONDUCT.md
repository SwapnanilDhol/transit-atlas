# Community code of conduct

Transit Atlas welcomes contributors regardless of background, identity, experience, or location.

Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable feedback, credit data and prior work, and keep technical disagreement focused on evidence. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
